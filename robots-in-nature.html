Robots in Nature

Human-Robot-Environment Interaction for Advanced Ecosystem Services

Aim

To explore the design of robotic systems that can deliver data-driven, precision ecosystem services to help address serious environmental issues & enrich everyday life

Objective

  • Explore interactions between humans, robots, and the environment
  • Develop robotic systems that respond to real-time data from the environment & precisely implement optimized solutions
  • Generate enhanced ecosystem services
